WordQuest

WordQuest is a friendly, single-page vocabulary game designed for young and beginning English learners.
It is designed to be short and confidence-building, just pick the definition you think is right.
WordQuest is easy and accessible for learners of all ages.
Start the quiz to test your vocabulary, see your score at the end, and restart anytime to practice more.

How to Play

How to play WordQuest:

1. Read the word shown at the top of the quiz area.
2. Look at the four answer choices below the word.
3. Select the one you think is correct (only one choice allowed).
4. Click the Submit Answer to go onto the next.
5. Continue through all five words, clicking submit after each guess.
6. At the end you will see your final score and can restart to play again.
